Welcome

At Voice of God Prophetic and Fire Ministries, we are dedicated to equipping and empowering believers to walk boldly in their divine purpose. Our programs are built on a foundation of faith, unity, and service, with each department playing a vital role in advancing the Kingdom of God.

Our Programs

Weekly Programs:

  • Sunday Service: Worship and Word Encounter
  • Tuesday Bible Study & Radio Programs: In-depth teaching and broadcasts
  • Hour of Mercy on the Mount (Wednesday): A time for divine healing and restoration
  • Workers' Meeting: Training and empowerment for church workers

Monthly Programs:

  • Prophetic Night: 1st Friday of every month—A night of prophecy and spiritual renewal

Annual Programs:

  • Department Anniversaries: Celebrating the growth and service of each church department
  • Church Anniversary: Commemorating the founding and milestones of the church
  • Church Harvest: A time for thanksgiving and celebration of God's blessings
  • Marathon Fasting & Prayer: From Christmas Day to New Year, seeking divine intervention and breakthrough for the year ahead

CHERUBIM & SERAPHIM VOICE OF GOD PROHETIC FIRE MINISTRIES is a place of deliverance and prophetic ministry, where faith in God is nurtured through the power of His Word and Spirit. We are committed to teaching the Bible and leading joyful worship, while embracing the transformative power of deliverance and prophecy. Our ministry serves people of all ages—children, youth, and adults—equipping them to walk in the fullness of God's calling and to serve others with love and grace. We invite you to experience the life-changing power of deliverance and prophecy as part of our spiritual family. You will find a welcoming atmosphere where each person is embraced and empowered. At Rise Church, we take seriously our mission: "To Know Jesus and make Him known; To Be Led by the Holy Spirit in all things; and to Proclaim God's Word to the world, bringing deliverance and prophetic insight to those in need."
