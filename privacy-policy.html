Privacy Policy

Introduction
Welcome to our Privacy Policy page. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we are committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and security of any personal information you provide while using our website and services. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you access our platform. By continuing to use our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy and agree to its terms.
Information We Collect
We collect various types of information to enhance your experience and improve our services. This may include:
  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing details, and any other details you voluntarily provide when creating an account or making a transaction.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with our website, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and time spent on our site.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to optimize site functionality and provide a better user experience.
Purpose of Data Collection
The information we collect is used for the following purposes:
  • To provide, operate, and maintain our website and services.
  • To personalize user experience and improve content relevance.
  • To process transactions and send updates regarding purchases or account activity.
  • To enhance security and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.
User Rights and Control Over Personal Data
We respect your right to access, modify, and delete your personal data. You may:
  • Request a copy of the personal data we have collected about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate or outdated information in your account settings.
  • Request the deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational constraints.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails.
Data Security and Protection
We take data security seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we encourage users to take precautions when sharing sensitive information online.
Third-Party Sharing and Confidentiality
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may only be shared in the following circumstances:
  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, or providing essential services, and only under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • To comply with legal obligations, respond to legal requests, or protect our rights and interests.
Authorized Communication Channels
For your security and privacy, all official communication should only be conducted through the approved channels listed on our website. Users are strictly prohibited from contacting or responding to any communication outside of these official channels. We are not responsible for any risks, fraud, or unauthorized disclosures that may arise from engaging with unverified sources. If you receive any suspicious messages or requests, please report them immediately through our designated contact page.
Changes to the Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changes in legal requirements, industry standards, or our data practices. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review the policy periodically. Your continued use of our services after changes have been implemented constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
Contact Information
If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us only through the official contact details provided on our website. Communication through any other means is strictly prohibited to maintain security and confidentiality.

CHERUBIM & SERAPHIM VOICE OF GOD PROPHETIC & FIRE MINISTRIES is a place of deliverance and prophetic ministry, where faith in God is nurtured through the power of His Word and Spirit. We are committed to teaching the Bible and leading joyful worship, while embracing the transformative power of deliverance and prophecy. Our ministry serves people of all ages—children, youth, and adults—equipping them to walk in the fullness of God's calling and to serve others with love and grace. We invite you to experience the life-changing power of deliverance and prophecy as part of our spiritual family. You will find a welcoming atmosphere where each person is embraced and empowered. At Rise Church, we take seriously our mission: "To Know Jesus and make Him known; To Be Led by the Holy Spirit in all things; and to Proclaim God's Word to the world, bringing deliverance and prophetic insight to those in need."
