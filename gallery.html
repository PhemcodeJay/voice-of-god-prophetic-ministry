CHERUBIM & SERAPHIM VOICE OF GOD PROPHETIC FIRE MINISTRIES is a place of deliverance and prophetic ministry, where faith in God is nurtured through the power of His Word and Spirit. We are committed to teaching the Bible and leading joyful worship, while embracing the transformative power of deliverance and prophecy. Our ministry serves people of all ages—children, youth, and adults—equipping them to walk in the fullness of God's calling and to serve others with love and grace.

We invite you to experience the life-changing power of deliverance and prophecy as part of our spiritual family. You will find a welcoming atmosphere where each person is embraced and empowered. At Rise Church, we take seriously our mission: "To Know Jesus and make Him known; To Be Led by the Holy Spirit in all things; and to Proclaim God's Word to the world, bringing deliverance and prophetic insight to those in need."
