Welcome

At Voice of God Prophetic and Fire Ministries, we are dedicated to equipping and empowering believers to walk boldly in their divine purpose. Our ministry is built on a foundation of faith, unity, and service, with each department playing a vital role in advancing the Kingdom of God.

Explore our key ministry departments:

  • Men's Ministry: A brotherhood of faith-driven men committed to spiritual leadership, personal growth, and community impact. This ministry strengthens men to lead with integrity in their families, careers, and the church.
  • Women's Ministry: A thriving community where women are empowered through faith, mentorship, and service. This ministry nurtures spiritual growth, strengthens families, and fosters a deep commitment to prayer and fellowship.
  • Youth Ministry: A dynamic space designed to ignite the passion of young believers. Through mentorship, discipleship, and engaging activities, this ministry equips the next generation to live with purpose and boldness in Christ.
  • Children's Ministry: A nurturing environment where the youngest members of our church grow in faith, learn biblical values, and develop a lasting love for Jesus.
  • Prayer Intercessors: A devoted team of warriors standing in the gap, interceding for the church, families, and the world. This ministry is committed to seeking divine intervention through unwavering prayer and spiritual warfare.
  • Choir Ministry: The heartbeat of worship in our church, leading the congregation into powerful, spirit-filled praise and adoration. This ministry ushers in God’s presence through anointed music and worship.
  • Army of Christ: A bold and fearless ministry focused on evangelism, outreach, and spiritual warfare. This group is on a mission to spread the gospel, break strongholds, and bring the light of Christ to the world.

These ministries work together to strengthen our church family, equip believers, and impact lives. We invite you to join us, get involved, and be a part of God’s transformative work as we continue to expand His Kingdom.

CHERUBIM & SERAPHIM VOICE OF GOD PROHETIC FIRE MINISTRIES is a place of deliverance and prophetic ministry, where faith in God is nurtured through the power of His Word and Spirit. We are committed to teaching the Bible and leading joyful worship, while embracing the transformative power of deliverance and prophecy. Our ministry serves people of all ages—children, youth, and adults—equipping them to walk in the fullness of God's calling and to serve others with love and grace. We invite you to experience the life-changing power of deliverance and prophecy as part of our spiritual family. You will find a welcoming atmosphere where each person is embraced and empowered. At Rise Church, we take seriously our mission: "To Know Jesus and make Him known; To Be Led by the Holy Spirit in all things; and to Proclaim God's Word to the world, bringing deliverance and prophetic insight to those in need."
