Bible Teachings

Salvation

Salvation is the central message of the Christian faith, referring to the deliverance of the soul from sin and eternal separation from God. It is a free gift from God, made possible through the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ.

By acknowledging Jesus as Lord and Savior, repenting of sins, and placing faith in His grace, individuals are forgiven and reconciled with God. Salvation brings not only eternal life but also transformation in the present life, empowering believers to live in victory over sin and to walk in God’s purpose.

Prophetic Ministry

Prophetic Ministry is a spiritual service that focuses on hearing and delivering messages from God to guide, encourage, and edify the church and individuals. It involves prophetic gifts such as prophecy, word of knowledge, and discernment.

It helps believers grow in intimacy with God and align with His purpose while also bringing warnings, correction, and spiritual growth.

Deliverance Ministry

Deliverance Ministry is focused on freeing individuals from spiritual oppression, bondage, or demonic influence. Through prayer, fasting, and the power of the Holy Spirit, individuals are set free from strongholds that affect their well-being.

This ministry emphasizes the authority of Jesus Christ to heal, restore, and bring victory over the forces of darkness.

Deliverance from Spiritual Warfare

Text: Isaiah 61:1-9 & Ephesians 6:10-16

Introduction

Beloved, today we are diving into a powerful message on deliverance from spiritual warfare. As believers in Christ, we are engaged in a constant battle—not against flesh and blood, but against spiritual forces that seek to destroy our peace, joy, and purpose.

1. The Anointing for Deliverance – Isaiah 61:1-3

“The Spirit of the Lord God is upon me, because the Lord has anointed me to preach good tidings to the poor; He has sent me to heal the brokenhearted, to proclaim liberty to the captives, and the opening of the prison to those who are bound...” (Isaiah 61:1-3)

Jesus quoted this prophecy in Luke 4:18, declaring that He came to bring deliverance. The anointing of the Holy Spirit empowers us to break free from every chain and oppression.

2. Putting on the Whole Armor of God – Ephesians 6:10-16

“Finally, my brethren, be strong in the Lord and in the power of His might. Put on the whole armor of God, that you may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil...” (Ephesians 6:10-16)
  • The Belt of Truth: Stand firm in God's Word.
  • The Breastplate of Righteousness: Be covered in Christ’s righteousness.
  • The Shoes of the Gospel of Peace: Carry and spread the Gospel.
  • The Shield of Faith: Defend against attacks of the enemy.
  • The Helmet of Salvation: Protect your mind with God’s truth.
  • The Sword of the Spirit: Use God’s Word as a weapon.

3. Victory in Jesus – Isaiah 61:7-9 & Ephesians 6:13

“Instead of your shame you shall have double honor, and instead of confusion they shall rejoice in their portion...” (Isaiah 61:7-9)

God promises that after the battle, we will experience *double honor*—a life of restoration, victory, and peace.

Conclusion

Beloved, spiritual warfare is real, but God has equipped us for victory. Through His anointing, the armor of God, and the power of His Word, we are destined to walk in victory.

“Therefore take up the whole armor of God, that you may be able to withstand in the evil day, and having done all, to stand.” (Ephesians 6:13)

Prayer

Father, we thank You for Your Word that equips us for victory. We put on the full armor of God today and stand firm in Your strength. We declare victory over every attack of the enemy. In Jesus' mighty name, Amen.

My Glory Must Shine

Text: Isaiah 60:1-10

Introduction

Beloved, today we are declaring a powerful prophetic word over our lives: "My Glory Must Shine!" The word of the Lord in Isaiah 60:1-10 calls us to arise and shine because the glory of God is upon us.

1. Arise and Shine – Isaiah 60:1

“Arise, shine; for your light has come, and the glory of the Lord is risen upon you.” (Isaiah 60:1)

God’s glory is upon you, and it is time for you to rise above the darkness that tries to keep you down.

2. The Darkness Shall Not Overcome You – Isaiah 60:2

“For behold, the darkness shall cover the earth, and deep darkness the people; but the Lord will arise over you, and His glory will be seen upon you.” (Isaiah 60:2)

God’s glory will shine brighter and overcome every darkness.

3. Nations Will Be Drawn to Your Light – Isaiah 60:3-4

“The Gentiles shall come to your light, and kings to the brightness of your rising.” (Isaiah 60:3-4)

When God’s glory shines in your life, it draws people to you.

2 Timothy 2:15 – "Study to show thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth."
