Welcome to a Place of Prophetic Power

Voice of God Prophetic & Fire Ministries

Cherubim and Seraphim Voice of God Prophetic & Fire Ministries is a dynamic extension of the Cherubim and Seraphim Church of Nigeria, founded in 1925 by Prophet Moses Orimolade Tunolase. Deeply rooted in the traditions of African Pentecostalism, our ministry carries a powerful legacy of prophetic anointing, divine healing, and supernatural deliverance.

Inspired by the celestial beings—Cherubim and Seraphim—our ministry is devoted to holiness, spiritual empowerment, and divine worship. Through prophetic revelation, intense intercession, and unwavering faith, we seek to break spiritual chains, transform lives, and guide people into a deeper relationship with God.

At Voice of God Prophetic & Fire Ministries, we are called to confront spiritual oppression, engage in strategic spiritual warfare, and release the fire of the Holy Spirit for revival and restoration. Our powerful worship experiences, fervent prayers, and life-changing deliverance services create an atmosphere where miracles happen, destinies are unlocked, and believers step into their God-ordained purpose.

A Global Prophetic and Deliverance Movement: With a divine mandate to spread God’s power across nations, we stand as a beacon of hope, healing, and transformation. Through prophetic insight and supernatural encounters, we break strongholds, restore lives, and lead individuals into divine alignment.

Our Mission: To raise a generation of believers walking in holiness, victory, and spiritual authority. Through prophetic declarations, deep intercession, and radical faith, we ignite revival, restore destinies, and empower lives.

Join the Movement: As a global force, we unite believers in the pursuit of deliverance, prophecy, and Holy Spirit fire. No matter where you are, you are welcome to experience God's presence, embrace His power, and step into your divine calling.

what we believe

Our Doctrine

We believe in one true God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—who reveals Himself through the Bible, His inspired Word. Jesus Christ, through His death and resurrection, offers salvation and eternal life to all who believe.

The Holy Spirit empowers believers with wisdom, spiritual gifts, and strength for victorious living.

We uphold holiness, prophetic ministry, deliverance, divine healing, and the power of prayer in spiritual warfare. As the body of Christ, the Church is a place of worship, fellowship, and service, equipping believers for a life of faith and the promise of eternity with God.

CHERUBIM & SERAPHIM VOICE OF GOD PROPHETIC & FIRE MINISTRIES is a place of deliverance and prophetic ministry, where faith in God is nurtured through the power of His Word and Spirit. We are committed to teaching the Bible and leading joyful worship, while embracing the transformative power of deliverance and prophecy. Our ministry serves people of all ages—children, youth, and adults—equipping them to walk in the fullness of God's calling and to serve others with love and grace. We invite you to experience the life-changing power of deliverance and prophecy as part of our spiritual family. You will find a welcoming atmosphere where each person is embraced and empowered.
